Lucy's Favorite Homemade Snacks:

Lucy loves to eat. It has been difficult purchasing Lucy treats because there is a lot of known named brands that she does not like. I decided to make her some treats myself and have had great results. Most of the recipes have the same ingredients or are somewhat made the same which is convienient. Here are some of her favorites.

Sardine Treats

One of Lucys favorites! This 3 ingredient treat is perfect if your cat is a fan of sardines. It's super easy to make and 1 batch will last a week.

Check out the recipe here!
Cookies and Milk Treats

I honestly have not made this recipe but it will be my next! I chose this one mainly because the kitten looks so adorable with his milk and cookies.

I was surprised to find out Lucy liked pumpkin. It made me happy because she was able to enjoy a festive treat last month.
