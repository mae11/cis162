About Lucy:

Welcome.
This is my cat Lucy. She is a playful but high maintence siamese cat. To be honest, I have always preferred dogs because cats seemed so evil. When I came across Lucy last year, all the negative feelings I had about cats changed. She was only a 1.5 months old and so shy. Now she is a full year old and wild in the streets!
She loves to play outside but can also be very lazy. She has a huge appetite but is also extremely picky. I figured I could share tips to other furparents with picky cats on her behalf.

Her name is Lucy but she responds to "Meow Meow."
This table is her go-to napping spot during the day. She loves to sleep in the sun.
She can be so playful and goofy. This photo is my favorite because of her little teeth.
Lucy being her divine self. I love how sweet she looks in this photo.
Lucy thankfully gets along with people/kids. This is my cousins baby who is only 1 and a half. It was her first time meeting a cat. Surprisingly, it was Lucys first time meeting a baby.
Lucy likes to try different fruits. I make sure to google which fruits are safe for cats.




Cats can't taste sweetness. They lack the receptor for sweetness.

Purring doesn't always mean a cat is happy. They also purr when they are sick, stressed, hurt, or giving birth.

Cats can jump up to six times its length.
